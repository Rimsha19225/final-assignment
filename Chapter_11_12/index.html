PIAIC

PRESIDENTAL INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing (PIAIC) as a privately funded, not-for-profit educational initiative. It was started by the President of Pakistan, Dr. Arif Alvi, alongside Kazi Rahat Ali and Zia Khan, with the goal of promoting education, research, and business opportunities in emerging technological fields.

The mission of PIAIC is to reshape Pakistan by revolutionizing education, research, and business by adopting latest, cutting-edge technologies. Experts are calling this the 4th industrial revolution. We want Pakistan to become a global hub for AI, data science, cloud native computing, edge computing, blockchain, augmented reality, and internet of things.

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet of Things

Cloud Native Computing:

Cloud Native Computing: This program trains students in open-source technologies such as Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and React Native. It also covers the development of microservices and software deployment techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence:

The AI program at PIAIC teaches foundational concepts in AI through programming languages like Python, Numpy, Pandas, TensorFlow 2.0, and Flask. It focuses on deploying practical AI solutions with real-life examples, preparing students for modern AI applications.

Blockchain:

This program focuses on understanding blockchains like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric). Students learn programming in Solidity and JavaScript with frameworks like Node.js. The curriculum covers the development of Distributed Apps (DApps) using Web3 and Truffle, understanding Blockchain-as-a-Service (BaaS), and creating Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) for crypto-tokens.

Internet of Things:

The Internet of Things program introduces students to open-source development technologies such as Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes. It focuses on efficiently developing microservices for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice control for device management.
