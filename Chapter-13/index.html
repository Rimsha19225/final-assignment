• Do Darya

    Do Darya in Karachi is a popular picnic spot known for its stunning seaside views and a variety of restaurants offering delicious seafood and local cuisine. Located along the Arabian Sea, it’s perfect for family outings and gatherings. Visitors enjoy sea breezes, beautiful sunsets, and tranquil vibes.

  • Clifton

    Clifton Park in Karachi is a peaceful recreational space ideal for families and nature lovers. Located near the iconic Clifton Beach, it offers greenery, walking paths, and seating areas for relaxation. The park is a great spot for picnics, leisurely strolls, and enjoying Karachi’s coastal breeze.

  • PAF Museum

    The PAF Museum in Karachi showcases Pakistan's aviation history, featuring aircraft, jets, and memorabilia from the Pakistan Air Force. Located on Shahrah-e-Faisal, it offers interactive exhibits, flight simulators, and historical displays. It's a popular destination to explore Pakistan’s air defense legacy.
